Why the name Deneb?

Deneb is a star in the constellation Cygnus (the Swan). It is very far away from earth -- approximately 1400 light years -- but appears as one of the 20 brightest stars to the naked eye. Deneb is approximately 60,000 times as bright as the sun. Now THAT would be a great neighborhood for solar PV!
