The Team

Chief Executive Officer

David G. Litt

Most of David's career has been as a partner in two major U.S. law firms, O'Melveny & Myers LLP and then Morrison & Foerster LLP. As a cross-border transactional lawyer, David developed significant experience in acquisition, development and financing of electric power and other infrastructure and technology projects. He also has over 15 years of experience with investments into Japan. In 2010 and 2011, Chambers Asia-Pacific rated David one of the top investment funds lawyers in Tokyo.  David is Vice Chair of the Energy and Environment Committee of the American Chamber of Commerce in Japan, and he also serves as a Guest Professor at the Faculty of Public Policy Management of Keio University.

David attended Yale College (BA, summa cum laude, 1984), the University of Chicago Law School (JD, with honors, 1988) and the Inter-University Center for Japanese Language Studies. After law school, he served as a judicial clerk for Justice Anthony M. Kennedy of the U.S. Supreme Court.

David has lived in Tokyo for over 12 years in total. He is a U.S. citizen and a permanent resident of Japan.

Chief Operating Officer

Dr. Steffen David

Steffen has been based in Tokyo for almost 10 years and is an experienced business consultant who specializes in developing Japanese and Asian companies. He is successful founder of several businesses in Japan and the region and has a broad experience in real estate. Through his various business ventures Steffen has identified and implemented aggressive growth strategies, optimized purchasing and operations and developed and steered turn-around strategies. Prior to pursuing his own business interests, Steffen was a management consultant with McKinsey & Company based out of their offices in Tokyo, Japan, and Munich, Germany.

Steffen holds a PhD (Dr. rer. oec.) from the University of Duisburg-Essen and a Masters in Business Administration and Industrial Engineering (Dipl.-Wi.-Ing.) from the University of Karlsruhe (TH).
